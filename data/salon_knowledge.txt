## BALAYAGE

Description

Balayage is a hand-painted highlighting technique that creates soft,
natural-looking color transitions.

Price

$150 - $250

Duration

3 Hours

Includes

• Consultation
• Lightening
• Toner
• Wash
• Blow Dry
• Styling

Best For

• Natural-looking highlights
• Low-maintenance color
• Dimensional hair

Lasts

3 to 4 Months

Maintenance

Touch-up every 12 to 16 weeks.

Benefits

• Natural appearance
• Less noticeable root growth
• Soft color blend
• Less maintenance than highlights

Aftercare

• Use sulfate-free shampoo.
• Avoid chlorine.
• Use heat protection.
• Deep condition weekly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does balayage damage hair?

Minimal damage when properly maintained.

Q: Can balayage be done on black hair?

Yes.

Q: Can balayage be done without bleach?

Sometimes, depending on the desired result.
